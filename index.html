Royal Enfield Bullet 350 Price

The Royal Enfield Bullet 350 is priced from Rs 1,54,327 for the Bullet Silver and Onyx Black colour versions of the base variant. The Black colour variant with the hand-painted gold pinstripes retails at Rs 1,61,385. The more premium Bullet 350 ES costs Rs 1,67,178 for all three colour options: Jet Black, Regal Red and Royal Blue. All prices are on-road Delhi.

Bullet 350 Specifications

The Royal Enfield Bullet 350 BS6 uses the same 346cc single-cylinder thumper as the Royal Enfield Classic 350 BS6. The motor produces 19.3PS at 5250rpm and 28Nm at 4000rpm. The gearbox continues to remain a 5-speed unit. The engine nestles inside a traditional single downtube frame linked to a telescopic front fork and twin rear shock absorbers. Braking hardware remains the same as the BS4 version, with a front 280mm disc and 153mm rear drum brake. Royal Enfield had introduced single-channel ABS back in 2019 itself.

Bullet 350 Design

Over the years, the Royal Enfield Bullet 350’s design has remained more or less the same. Royal Enfield has kept it intact as it evokes the sense of nostalgia associated with basic no-nonsense British motorcycles. The round headlamp with chrome nacelle, the iconic ‘Tiger lamps’, the classic-styled fuel tank, and the single-piece quilted leather seat are all in tune with the bike’s retro look.

Jawa 42 Price

The 42 is priced from Rs. 1.69 - 1.83 Lakh(Aprox.)

Jawa 42 Specifications

Powering the Jawa 42 is a liquid-cooled, 293cc engine that generates 27.33PS of power and 27.03Nm of torque. It’s mated to a 6-speed gearbox. Braking on the Jawa 42 is done by 280mm front disc and 153mm drum at the rear, and an optional 240mm rear disc. Single-channel ABS comes standard. The bike is sprung on a telescopic fork and twin-gas charged rear shocks.

Jawa 42 Variants

The Jawa 42 is sold in three variants -- Single Disc, Dual Disc and the latest 42 2.1. The first two variants are available in six colour options -- Lumous Lime, Halley’s Teal, Comet Red, Galactic Green, Nebula Blue, and Straight Blue, while the 42 2.1 comes in three exclusive shades.

Jawa 42 Features

Jawa has kept a minimal approach. It has equipped the 42 with an analogue speedometer, fuel guage, and a small digital patch for the odometer readings while skipping the gear indicator and tachometer. Even the headlight and indicators use bulbs to keep the retro appeal flowing.
